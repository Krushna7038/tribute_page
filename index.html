Mahendra Singh Dhoni

About The MSD


MS Dhoni, in full Mahendra Singh Pansingh Dhoni, has born on July 7, 1981, in Ranchi, Bihar (now Jharkhand), India.


He made his profession as 'Indian International Cricketer'. Till 1998, he played for the school cricket team and club cricket. Later he was selected for the Central Coal Fields Limited(CCL) team.


He made his debut for the Indian cricket team on 23 December 2004 in an ODI against Bangladesh and played his first test a year later against Sri Lanka. In 2007 , he became the captain of the ODI side before taking over in all formats by 2008.


Dhoni was selected for India A team for a tour of Zimbabwe and Kenya. At Harare Sports Club, against Zimbabwe, Dhoni had 7 catches and 4 stumpings in the match.


On the 31st October, 2005 Dhoni broke the record of highest runs scored by a wicket keeper by securing 183 runs against Sri Lanka which was played at Jaipur.


Mahi, MSD, Captain Cool, Thala are the nick names of the Mahendra Singh Dhoni.


MS Dhoni's unbeaten knock of 91 runs in the final was crucial in India's victory.


In 2011, he has conferred an honorary rank of lieutenant-colonel in the Indian Territorial Army for his contribution to cricket. In August 2019, he completed a two-week stint with the Army in Jammu and Kashmir region.


MS Dhoni is the co-owner of Ranchi Rays (Ranchi-based Hockey Club) along with Sahara India Pariwar. Also he is the co-owner of Chennaiyin FC(Chennai-based football club) and Supersport World Championship team, Mahi Racing Team India.


In February 2016, Dhoni launched his brand 'SEVEN'. He owns the footwear of the brand is the brand ambassador of his company.


MS Dhoni is awarded by different awards like in 2018, he received India's third-highest civilian award- Padma Bhushan, in 2019 Padma Shri, in 2008,2009, he was awarded the ICC ODI Player Of the Year.


For 2007-2008, he received India's highest honour given for achievement in sports - Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna.
